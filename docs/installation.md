# Prerequisites
- An iOS/iPadOS device [with a passcode](https://support.apple.com/en-us/119586) running iOS 17.1 or later.
- A computer running at least...
  - Windows 8
  - macOS High Sierra
  - an up-to-date Linux distro
- An Apple Account a.k.a. Apple ID.
- A Wi-Fi connection. (Cellular won't work)
- [LocalDevVPN](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/localdevvpn/id6755608044) installed on your iDevice
- (Windows Only) [iTunes.](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9pb2mz1zmb1s?hl=en-GB&gl=CA)
- (Linux Only) [usbmuxd.](https://github.com/libimobiledevice/usbmuxd/releases) (Might already be available on your machine)
- [iLoader](https://iloader.app) on your machine

# Installing SideStore
1. Connect your device by cable into your machine.
2. If prompted trust the computer.
3. Open iloader
4. Sign in with your Apple Account. This is used to sign the app.
5. Select your device.
6. Click 'Install SideStore (Stable)'.

## On your iDevice
=== "iOS 18+"
	1. Open Settings.
	2. Go to General > VPN & Device Management.
	3. Tap Trust and select Allow & Restart.
	4. Enter your passcode.
	5. Go to Privacy & Security.
	6. Scroll to the bottom and select Developer Mode.
	7. After your device restarts open LocalDevVPN.
	8. Tap Connect.
	9. Open SideStore.
	10. Sign into the same Apple Account you used for iloader
	11. Go to My Apps
	12. Tap the + and select the .ipa file for LabelWorkshop
=== "iOS 17.1+"
	1. Open Settings.
	2. Go to General > VPN & Device Management.
	3. Tap Trust and confirm it.
	5. Go to Privacy & Security.
	6. Scroll to the bottom and select Developer Mode.
	7. After your device restarts open LocalDevVPN.
	8. Tap Connect.
	9. Open SideStore.
	10. Sign into the same Apple Account you used for iloader
	11. Go to My Apps
	12. Tap the + and select the .ipa file for LabelWorkshop
